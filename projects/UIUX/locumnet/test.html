LocumNet Portal Redesign

A redesign of the LocumNet Portal job listing website, improving its aesthetics and functionality.

Project Overview

The previous design of the LocumNet Portal job listing website felt cluttered and outdated. The redesign aimed to enhance its professionalism, improve visual hierarchy, update aesthetics, ensure better readability, and maintain consistency.

Design Goals

  • Enhance Professionalism
  • Improve Visual Hierarchy
  • Modern Aesthetic
  • Better Readability
  • Consistency

Process

The redesign process involved:

  1. Analyzing the existing design
  2. Wireframing & Prototyping in Figma
  3. Applying New Visual Elements (Typography, Color Schemes, UI Components)
  4. Testing & Refinements

Before & After

The redesign improved various aspects of the LocumNet Portal:

Hero Section

Before: Cluttered banner with excessive text and small icons.

After: Sleek, minimal design featuring a welcoming image and focused job search area.

Search Functionality

Before: Large, distracting banner overshadowed the search bar.

After: Well-structured, prominently placed search bar with clear input fields.

Reflection

The redesign reinforced the importance of visual hierarchy, whitespace, and consistency in UI design. It improved user experience by making job searching easier and more intuitive.

Tools Used

  • Figma
  • Adobe Photoshop (optional)

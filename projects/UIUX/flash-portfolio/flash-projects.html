Flash Interactive Projects (2008 - 2013)

A Journey Through Creativity and Innovation

During my diploma and bachelor's degree, I worked extensively on Flash Interactive projects, combining UI/UX design, animation, and coding using ActionScript 2.0. These projects were my passion, allowing me to bring creative ideas to life through engaging digital experiences. While Flash is no longer widely used, the knowledge and skills I gained continue to influence my work as a UI/UX designer and front-end developer.

Projects Overview

Below are some of my Flash Interactive projects:

Personal Portfolio Site (Interactive Flash Design)

  • Designed and developed a fully interactive portfolio showcasing my work with dynamic transitions and engaging animations.

Interactive Comic

  • Created an immersive storytelling experience with animated panels, sound effects, and interactive choices to enhance user engagement.

Interactive Documentary

Flash Games

  • Developed a series of interactive games, incorporating UI/UX principles to enhance player experience, smooth animations, and engaging mechanics.
